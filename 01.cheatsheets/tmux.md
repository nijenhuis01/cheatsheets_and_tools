## Thumx Cheatsheet

### commands
|Command|Description|
|---|---|
|tmux|Start tmux|
|ctrl+b|tmux: default prefix|
|prefix c|tmux: new window|
|prefix 1|tmux: switch to window (1)|
|prefix shift+%|tmux: split pane vertically|
|prefix shift+"|tmux: split pane horizontally|
|prefix ->|tmux: switch to the right pane|
